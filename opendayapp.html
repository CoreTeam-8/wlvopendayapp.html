Join us for our on-campus undergraduate open day

Discover Your Future

We're ready to welcome you to our next University of Wolverhampton undergraduate open day - on campus, in person. It's the perfect way to help you decide if studying with us is your perfect fit.

Look around our facilities, tour our campuses, meet the lecturers, and see the student accommodation. You can also drop by the Students' Union and talk to our experts about money, undergraduate courses, careers, and more.

Find Your Campus and Programme

To find the correct campus for your subject, simply select your subject area of interest from the drop-down menu below. To make the best use of your time, head to the campus where your main subject will be taught first.

If you're still not sure where to go, give us a call! 0800 953 3222 or email: enquiries@wlv.ac.uk

Can't Attend?

If you can't attend our undergraduate open day, we offer virtual tours, where you can explore our campuses and facilities from the comfort of your own home. We also offer campus tours, which give you the chance to see our campuses in person on a different date.

Get in Touch

Have a question about our undergraduate open days? We are happy to help. Simply get in touch with our team, who will assist with your query.

City Campus (Wolverhampton)

Wulfruna Street, Wolverhampton, WV1 1LY

The City Campus is the main campus of the University of Wolverhampton, located in the heart of Wolverhampton. It houses the Faculty of Science and Engineering, Faculty of Arts, and Faculty of Social Sciences.

Walsall Campus

Gorway Road, Walsall, WS1 3BD

The Walsall Campus is home to the Faculty of Education, Health, and Wellbeing. It features state-of-the-art sports facilities, including a gym, swimming pool, and sports hall.

Telford Campus

Shifnal Road, Priorslee, Telford, TF2 9NT

The Telford Campus specializes in business, computing, and engineering courses. It offers modern facilities and a supportive learning environment.

University of Wolverhampton at Stafford

Blackheath Lane, Stafford, ST18 0YG

The Stafford campus offers courses in nursing, midwifery, and health-related disciplines. It is equipped with simulation suites and clinical skills labs.

University Building

Our university campus is home to several state-of-the-art buildings, including:

  • ML Building - Faiths facilities are located at ML Building, along with research facilities for Health and Wellbeing, and several administrative departments.
  • MX: The Housman Building - The University's central administration hub, MX Building is a base for several administrative departments. It also houses a 190 seat lecture theatre, plus several teaching rooms, and is the main teaching area for City Campus North.
  • MH: Mary Seacole Building - The purpose-built Mary Seacole Building includes newly developed skills centres, simulated hospital wards, large IT suites and modern social learning spaces.
  • MC: Millennium City Building - One of the main social spaces on campus, Millennium City Building (MC) includes our main dining areas, and Starbucks branch.

Student Union

The Student Union is the heart of campus life, offering a variety of services and activities:

  • Cafeteria - A place to grab a bite and socialize.
  • Clubs and Societies - Join one of our many student organizations.
  • Student Services - Support for academic and personal needs.
  • Events - Regular events, including concerts, workshops, and more.

Sports Facilities

Our sports facilities are among the best in the region, including:

  • Gym Training
  • Footsall Fields
  • Indoor Sports Hall - For basketball, volleyball, and badminton.
  • Fitness Center - Fully equipped gym with personal trainers available.
