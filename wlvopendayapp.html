University Building

Our university campus is home to several state-of-the-art buildings, including:

  • ML Building - Faiths facilities are located at ML Building, along with research facilities for Health and Wellbeing, and several administrative departments..
  • MX: The Housman Building - The University's central administration hub, MX Building is a base for several administrative departments. It also houses a 190 seat lecture theatre, plus several teaching rooms, and is the main teaching area for City Campus North.
  • MH: Mary Seacole Building - The purpose-built Mary Seacole Building includes newly developed skills centres, simulated hospital wards, large IT suites and modern social learning spaces.
  • MC: Millennium City Building -One of the main social spaces on campus, Millennium City Building (MC) includes our main dining areas, and Starbucks branch..
